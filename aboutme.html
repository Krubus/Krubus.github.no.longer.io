Brief Story

Jacob is an aspiring cinematographer with an interest in editing. His parents split up when he was young but he now lives with his stepdad (who works in architectural visualisation) and mother (who is a painter). Jacob was born in Kozienice, Poland in 1999. In 2007 he moved to England and has lived there ever since. After primary education in Barnet and secondary education in Hertfordshire, Jacob took a film production course at the University of Winchester in 2017. From September 2020, Jacob intends on doing a masters in digital media practice at the same University.

Interests

Jacob has hobbies in a wide range of fields. He very recently learned HTML and basic CSS and JavaScript to code this website as part of his film production course; he will continue learning how to code. He has been teaching himself to play the piano for over 12 years, he also started learning the guitar 4 years ago. He has dabbled in a number of activities over the years including collage drawing, stop-motion animation, live-streaming, audio engineering and many more. He has learned German to a B2 CEFR level, Japanese to a B1 level and speaks Polish and English natively. He began learning Macedonian in 2020 to talk to friends abroad. Jacob interests himself in photography, design, colour theory and almost everything in the realm of interesting visual experiences.
